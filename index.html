About The Conference

Computer Vision DevCon 2020 (CVDC) is a 2 days influential conference on computer vision. Despite computer vision being such a specific subarea of ​​artificial intelligence & machine learning, the volume of problems derived from this approach is very extensive. CVDC plans to uncover those approaches and bring out the latest research and advancements in this field.
